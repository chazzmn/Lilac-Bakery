Our Story

Two self-taught bakers, one little bakery.

Lilac Bakery started the way the best ones do — at home, with too much flour and a lot of trial and error. We taught ourselves to bake, fell hard for it, and turned a corner shop on Cowick Street into a bakery the neighbourhood actually queues for.

Everything is made by us, on site: loaves leavened slowly with our own sourdough starter, viennoiserie laminated by hand, cakes baked to order. Nothing arrives frozen on a lorry. We get in before the sun, and we’re proud of every shelf.
